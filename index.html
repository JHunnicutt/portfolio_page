HTML Visualizer

I wanted to complete an exercize making a web app that transformed raw text complete with html tags into fully styled HTML. The process behind it uses a textarea section with JavaScript to inject code into a companion div.

"BOOK: A Revolutionary Form of Entertainment"

This came from a freeCodeCamp assignment to design a product landing page. I had the idea of putting on a comical spin on it. I would develop a page for a fake product titled "BOOK." The satire would be directed at our relience on our multiple devices in order to derive entertaiment. The company in question would be using this page to unveil a revolutionary new product, one comprised of paper pages and doesn't require power. This one was fun for me to put together. I've very proud of the logo that I designed for this.

Podcast Survey

Another freeCodeCamp challenge. Me, being a podcast fanatic, designed a survey form for a fictional podcast company inquring about the listening habits of its patrons.
